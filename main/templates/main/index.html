ABOUT US

VisionX(VisionX.world) is an ecosystem for promotixng AI development and landing AI solutions in a de-centralized way. It consists of AI technology and product development (VisionX.llc), AI marketplace (AI-mart.org) and AI community (VisionX.org).

The VisionX AI ecosystem, unlike traditional operations based on efforts from a single entity or organization, aims to utilize Blockchain technologies to conduct a de-centralized global operation and make AI applications highly efficient and sustainable.

Our Mission

We built a bridge for industries and enterprises to realize cross-industry collaboration, with the goal of creating high-quality AI solutions and leading the way into Industry 4.0.

In the future, we are going to be able to serve thousands of industries and offer millions of AI solutions with a vast and ever-expanding dataset.

Starting at defect detection, we are expanding into robot pick and place, predictive maintenance, smart voice interaction, supply chain and much more.
